# Ironsworn Character Creation — Baseline (No Skill)

## Session Transcript

---

**Player:** I want to make an Ironsworn character. She's a former soldier who left her war-band after a battle went wrong. I'm thinking something with iron as her highest stat, maybe a sword, and she has a hound. For her background vow, she wants to find the commander who betrayed her unit.

---

**GM:** Great concept — a disgraced warrior driven by a deep oath of vengeance. Let's build her out.

**First, let's give her a name.** In the Ironlands, names tend to be short and hard-edged. Something like Kael, Mira, Skara, or Voss. What feels right to you?

**Player:** I like Kael — or maybe something more feminine. How about Kael sounds too male. Let's go with **Skara**.

**GM:** Skara it is. Good name — sounds like someone who's survived things.

---

### Stats

**GM:** In Ironsworn, you assign the array **3, 2, 2, 1, 1** across your five stats: Edge, Heart, Iron, Shadow, and Wits.

- **Iron** — your might and physical power in direct combat
- **Edge** — speed, precision, and finesse
- **Heart** — courage, empathy, and force of will
- **Shadow** — cunning, deception, and stealth
- **Wits** — perception, craft, and knowledge

You said you want Iron as her highest. Given her background as a soldier who fought in close battles and now carries an oath of vengeance, here's a suggested spread:

| Stat   | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Iron   | 3     |
| Heart  | 2     |
| Wits   | 2     |
| Edge   | 1     |
| Shadow | 1     |

Iron 3 reflects her battlefield training. Heart 2 fits someone who held loyalty to her unit above all and who feels the betrayal deeply. Wits 2 — she's a veteran; she reads terrain and fights smart. Edge and Shadow at 1 — she's no rogue or skirmisher.

Does that feel right, or do you want to adjust?

**Player:** That's perfect. She's a brawler and a loyal soldier, not a schemer.

---

### Starting Resources

**GM:** All characters start with:

- **Momentum:** 2 (resets to 2 after burning)
- **Health:** 5
- **Spirit:** 5
- **Supply:** 5

These will shift during play. You're ready to go on all of those.

---

### Assets

**GM:** You choose **3 assets** at character creation. You mentioned a sword and a hound — those both map cleanly to Ironsworn assets. Let's lock those in and pick a third.

**Asset 1 — Hound**

The Hound companion is a loyal war-dog. Its first ability (free at creation) lets you add +1 when you make moves assisted by your hound — tracking, hunting, detecting threats.

**Asset 2 — Ironclad**

You mentioned a sword, but Ironsworn doesn't have a "sword" asset specifically. The asset that best fits a heavily armed, armor-wearing veteran is **Ironclad** — you wear heavy armor, and the first ability lets you inflict +1 harm and ignore one armor-related penalty on a weak hit when you clash in melee. This fits her soldier identity better than a generic weapon pick.

Alternatively, if you want to represent the sword specifically, **Sword** is actually a valid asset in some editions — it gives +1 momentum on a strong hit when you Strike. I'll include it as the sword asset if that's available in your edition.

**Player:** Let's use the Sword asset if it exists, and Ironclad for the heavy soldier feel.

**GM:** Done. We'll use **Sword** (momentum on strong hit strikes), **Hound** (companion), and **Ironclad** (heavy armor, melee resilience) as our three assets. Each starts with only the first ability unlocked.

---

### Background Vow

**GM:** Your background vow is the oath that defines who Skara is at the start of play. You said she wants to **find the commander who betrayed her unit**.

This is personal, dangerous, and far-reaching — that makes it an **Epic** vow. It won't be resolved quickly; it will shape the entire campaign. We'll also give her a nearer-term **Formidable** vow to pursue in the early sessions — something like *"Discover what happened to the survivors of my war-band"* or *"Find proof of the commander's treachery."*

**Player:** Yes — the epic vow is finding and confronting the commander. The formidable vow is tracking down the survivors to learn what they know.

**GM:** Perfect.

- **Background Vow (Epic):** *"I will find Commander [Name] and make him answer for the betrayal of my war-band."*
- **Near Vow (Formidable):** *"I will find the survivors of my war-band and learn the truth of what happened."*

Both tracks start at 0 ticks and are incomplete.

---

### Bonds

**GM:** Starting bonds represent the people and communities Skara is connected to. You begin with **0 bond progress** — bonds are earned through play via the *Forge a Bond* move. As a woman who just left her war-band under terrible circumstances, she may be starting fairly isolated. That fits narratively.

**Player:** Yeah, she's alone. She had her unit, and now she doesn't. The hound is probably the only one she trusts.

**GM:** That's a strong starting position — isolated, haunted, but driven. Let's note her hound as a narrative bond-in-waiting. Mechanically, bonds start at 0.

---

### Character Summary

**GM:** Here's Skara as she stands:

**Name:** Skara

**Vows:**

**Player:** This looks great. Where do we start?

**GM:** Skara is somewhere at the edge of the Ironlands, away from the warbands and the old campaigns. She has her hound, her sword, and a name she's hunting. The world is cold and the paths are unmarked.
