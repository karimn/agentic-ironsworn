# Baseline Character Generation — No Skill

**Eval**: eval-2-random-character  
**Condition**: WITHOUT skill (baseline)  
**Date**: 2026-04-26

---

## Prompt Received

"Just generate me a random Ironsworn character. Surprise me."

---

## Approach

No structured skill framework was used. Character was generated from general knowledge of the Ironsworn ruleset (Shawn Tomkin, Ironsworn TTRPG). Decisions were made based on:

- Thematic coherence (building an archetype that feels grounded)
- Mechanical soundness (stat distribution, asset selection)
- Narrative hooks (vow + inciting incident that create immediate tension)

---

## Decisions Made

### Name

Chose **Maren** — a short, weathered name with a Norse/Iron Age feel, suitable for the grim Ironlands. Gender-flexible but presented here as a woman of the Havens who took to the wilds.

### Character Concept

Maren is a **wilderness scout and outrider** — someone who ranges ahead of settlements and caravans, reading the land, tracking beasts and threats, and living on the margins of the Ironlands' sparse civilization. She's capable, self-reliant, and quietly carries a wound from her past.

### Stat Assignment (3/2/2/1/1)

Ironsworn stats: **Edge, Heart, Iron, Shadow, Wits**

- **Edge 3** — Maren is quick and precise. Edge governs ranged attacks and initiative. As an archer and outrider, this is her primary strength.
- **Heart 2** — She has genuine bonds with a few people and her horse. Heart governs companion moves and social connection.
- **Iron 2** — She can stand in a fight when needed. Iron governs head-on melee combat and tests of strength.
- **Wits 1** — She reads the land better than books, but formal reasoning and cunning aren't her strongest suit.
- **Shadow 1** — She's not a deceiver or infiltrator. She prefers open spaces to shadows.

*Rationale*: Edge 3 as primary stat reinforces the scout/archer archetype. Splitting 2/2 between Heart and Iron gives her emotional depth (bonds, companions) alongside physical resilience. Low Wits and Shadow keep her from feeling like a "skill monkey" and add clear weaknesses.

### Assets (3 selected)

1. **Ranger** (Path asset) — Maren can Resupply by hunting and foraging, and has bonuses when moving through the wilds. This directly defines her lifestyle and profession.

2. **Horse** (Companion asset) — Her horse, named **Cinder**, is a dun-coated mare with a scarred flank from an old wolf attack. The horse has health 5 and provides bonuses to travel and combat. Maren has cared for Cinder for three years; the bond is one of the most stable relationships in her life.

3. **Archer** (Path asset) — Maren trained as a settlement archer before leaving. She can strike at range, reroll dice on ranged strikes, and pin or harass foes. Pairs mechanically with Edge 3.

*Rationale*: Ranger + Archer creates a coherent physical identity (wilderness survival, ranged combat). Horse adds a narrative anchor — a companion who can be threatened, creating emotional stakes — and mechanical travel utility.

### Background Vow (Rank: Extreme)

**"I will find and destroy the warband that burned Harrow's Crossing, so that its survivors may finally grieve and rebuild."**

Three years ago, the small settlement of Harrow's Crossing — where Maren's family lived — was razed by a roving warband flying no banner. Maren was away ranging at the time. She returned to ash and scattered survivors. She swore on iron that she would find those responsible and end them. She has been tracking them across the Ironlands ever since. Progress: **0/40 ticks** (Extreme vow, starts empty).

### Inciting Incident (Rank: Dangerous)

**"A survivor of Harrow's Crossing has been found — barely alive, captive in a raider camp two days' ride east. She is the only person who saw the warband's leader's face."**

A traveling herbalist brought word to Maren at dusk: old Pela, who everyone assumed dead, was seen alive in a raider encampment near the Thornwood. If Pela dies or is moved, the lead goes cold. Maren must ride hard and act fast. Progress: **0/20 ticks** (Dangerous vow, starts empty).

### Starting Resources (Ironsworn Defaults)

- Health: 5
- Spirit: 5
- Supply: 5
- Momentum: +2 (starting value; max 10, reset 2)
- Debilities: none

## Character Summary

**Maren** is a lean, weathered woman in her early thirties, with crow-black hair cut short and a longbow she's carried since she was sixteen. She smells faintly of pine smoke and horse. She doesn't talk much about Harrow's Crossing, but the oath is carved into a piece of iron she keeps around her neck. Cinder, her dun mare, is more patient with her than any person has been.

She has two threads pulling at her: the slow, grinding work of tracking a warband across years of wilderness, and the sudden urgency of a survivor who might finally give her a face to find. She rides east at first light.

## Notes on Process (Baseline Evaluation)

Without a skill framework, the generation process relied entirely on:

- Recalled Ironsworn rules (stat names, starting values, asset categories)
- Freeform creative judgment for thematic fit
- No validation step for asset names or rules accuracy
- No oracle rolls — choices were made editorially, not procedurally

Potential issues in a blind test:
- Asset names ("Ranger," "Archer") may not exactly match official Ironsworn asset card titles
- No check was made against the official asset list
- Stat assignment rationale was narrative-first rather than rules-reference-first
- Progress track ranks were chosen by feel, not by a systematic table reference
